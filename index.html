Internships

  • Quantcast
  • TCL Research America
  • ASU

Quantcast is an adtech company that specializes in using big data to produce custom-targeted audience segments to advertisers. To do so, the company processes thirty pentabytes of data daily.

At Quantcast, Alfred worked on the EDGE team to develop a distributed system that could support the transfer of terrabytes of data across hundreds of computers in data centers both in the United States and internationally. Through this process, Alfred has become familiar with working with distirbuted file systems, parallelization, and writing systems that scale at an industry level. He has also become familiar with automated deployment tools such as Jenkins and Puppet, and understands the power of well written and robust testing.

Alfred worked at Quantcast from June 2015 to September 2015.

At TCL Research America, Alfred worked with video alignment software.

Alfred worked there from June 2014 to September 2014.

Alfred worked in the BioDesign lab at ASU under Professor Ye JiePing . There, he looked for patterns in the STAR*D depression study.

Alfred worked there from June 2013 to July 2013.
